# werewolf# 🐺 Werewolf Game

A free multiplayer Werewolf game for 60 players divided into 4 groups.

## Features

✨ **Multiplayer Support**
- 60 players maximum
- 4 independent game groups (15 players each)
- Real-time player tracking

🎮 **Game Roles**
- **Villagers**: Identify and vote out werewolves
- **Werewolves**: Hunt villagers during the night
- **Seer**: Reveals a player's role each night
- **Doctor**: Protects a player from elimination

📊 **Game Mechanics**
- Day/Night phases
- Voting system
- Role assignment
- Status tracking

## How to Play

1. Open the game link in your browser
2. Click "Start New Game" or "Join Game"
3. Enter your name
4. Select a group (1-4)
5. Click "Join Game" to start playing!

## Game Rules

**Day Phase:**
- All players discuss who might be a werewolf
- Players vote to eliminate someone

**Night Phase:**
- Werewolves choose a villager to eliminate
- Seer reveals a player's role
- Doctor protects a player

**Win Conditions:**
- Villagers Win: All werewolves are eliminated
- Werewolves Win: Werewolves equal or outnumber villagers
